Experience Layer — the front door. Chat, APIs, file uploads. No training required.

Control Layer — the orchestrator. It decides which AI agents run, in what order, and when to pause for human judgment.

Intelligence Layer — the brain. AI that reasons over your delivery graph — your decisions, your patterns, your rules — not generic internet knowledge.

Knowledge Layer — the memory. It holds relationships between your requirements, decisions, teams, and artefacts. Intelligent search that finds meaning, not keywords.

Platform & Governance Layer — the foundation. Every decision logged with a full audit trail. Every output traceable to its input.

And here is the principle that governs everything: the Governance Lock.

The AI reasons. The agents execute within their boundaries. But before any output crosses a system boundary — before a ticket is created, a document is published, a pipeline is triggered — it passes through a human approval gate.

Intelligence is AI-driven. Authority is always Human. That is the Governance Lock — and it is an exoskeleton for talent, not a replacement."
